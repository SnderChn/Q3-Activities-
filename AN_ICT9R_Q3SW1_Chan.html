Brief Introduction

Vikings is a historical drama television series with a Norse mythology theme, following the legendary tales of the Viking Ragnar Lothbrok, one of the best-known legendary Norse heroes and notorious as the scourge of England and France.

Some of my favorite characters

Ragnar Lothbrok

Ragnar is a legendary Norse hero and warrior, who rises to become a legendary king and conqueror. He is known for his cunning tactics and leadership skills.

Lagertha

Lagertha is a legendary shieldmaiden and the wife of Ragnar. She is known for her bravery and skills in battle, and for being a powerful and independent woman.

Bjorn Ironside

Bjorn is the son of Ragnar and Lagertha, and one of the most legendary Viking warriors. He is known for his strength and fearless attitude in battle.
